Optimize Impact, Sustainability, and Reach.

üí° Introduction (LDC Context)

This simulator models a Revolving Fund for sanitation in Least Developed Countries (LDCs). We've pre-loaded a scenario for Malawi:

  • Capital: $5M Loan + $500k Grant
  • Goal: Sustainable growth to close the SDG6 Gap.
  • Challenge: Balance affordability (Low Interest) with Fund Survival (Solvency).

üìö How to Run

  1. 1. Auto-Load: The model is fetching real-time data for Malawi...
  2. 2. Run Model: Click "Calculated Model" to see the projection.
  3. 3. Optimize: Tweaking Interest Rates affects demand. Tweaking Grant Support affects projected fund life.

üîÑ Theory of Change (Revised)

This model uses a Generalized Repayment Engine. It prioritizes Investor Repayment above all else.

If the fund forecasts a cash shortfall (based on your chosen Term), it will automatically slow down lending or hibernate to preserve liquidity.

Your Mission: Find the balance where the fund grows fast enough to reach the poor, but slow enough to guarantee the Investor gets their money back.

üöÄ How to Use

  • Step 1: Select a Country (loads real economic data).
  • Step 2: Adjust the "Levers" (Interest Rates, Loan Terms, Grant %).
  • Step 3: Watch the Charts & Scorecard. Avoid "Insolvency"!
